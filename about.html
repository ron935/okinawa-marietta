Who We Are

Okinawa Hibachi & Sushi

Located on Dallas Highway in Marietta, Okinawa Hibachi & Sushi brings the flavors of Japan to the heart of Cobb County. As part of the Okinawa family of restaurants across Georgia, we are proud to carry on a tradition of quality, freshness, and hospitality.

Our chefs prepare every dish with care — from hand-rolled sushi using the freshest fish to sizzling hibachi plates cooked right before your eyes. Whether it's a quick lunch or a special dinner outing, we make every meal memorable.

We believe great food brings people together, and our warm, welcoming atmosphere is designed to make you feel right at home.
